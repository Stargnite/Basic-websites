About me

Sobayo Tobi by name. A citizen of Nigeria from Ogun State. I stay at Lagos and I'm a software developer with about 2 years experience. I develop all kinds of software, apps, games, websites and more... I am also good at mixing and mastering engineering that uses Cubase and FL studio as his favourite software. I am available for any kind of open source contribution. Hit me up.

I can also help in improving your start-up like building your audience, creating a community and document the project journey.. I am ready to work anywhere around the world. Hit me up and let's discuss.
